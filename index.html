Welcome to Siang Tian Elementary School

Erlin Township, Changhua County, Taiwan

Siang Tian Elementary School is a rural ecological school located in Erlin Township, Changhua County, Taiwan. Surrounded by beautiful farmland and nature, we provide students with meaningful learning experiences both inside and outside the classroom.
