Welcome to Siang Tian Elementary School

Erlin Township, Changhua County, Taiwan

Siang Tian Elementary School is a rural ecological school located in Erlin Township, Changhua County, Taiwan. Surrounded by beautiful farmland and nature, we provide students with meaningful learning experiences both inside and outside the classroom.

Our 特色 Activities

⚽ Soccer

Students develop teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness through training and competitions.

🧗 Rock Climbing

Students challenge themselves while building courage, confidence, and problem-solving skills.

🚲 Unicycle

Students improve balance, coordination, and perseverance through regular practice and performances.

🎵 Harmonica

Students develop musical talents, creativity, and self-expression through harmonica learning and performances.

🌾 Rice Field Learning

Students experience planting and harvesting rice while learning about agriculture, sustainability, and local culture.

🌍 Bilingual Education

Students strengthen their English communication skills and develop a broader understanding of the world.

Our Mission

To provide a caring and inspiring learning environment where every child can learn, grow, and achieve their full potential.

Our Vision

To cultivate confident, responsible, compassionate, and lifelong learners who contribute positively to their community and the world.
