🌍 Sister School Program

Our Bilingual Education program proudly extends into global partnerships. Through our Sister School Program, students at Siang Tian Elementary School connect with international peers to exchange cultural traditions, practice real-world language skills, and collaborate on shared learning experiences that broaden their global horizons.

Recent Partnership Activities

👋 Activity 1: Getting to Know Our Peers

Students from both schools recorded creative introduction messages, sharing details about their names, hobbies, daily school schedules, and local life in Changhua County to build an initial cultural connection.

🍲 Activity 2: Local Culinary Traditions

A fun video presentation highlighting favorite local Taiwanese dishes and school lunches. Students explored culinary similarities and differences with our international partner school peers.

🏫 Activity 3: Walking Through Siang Tian

Our students guided their sister school friends on a completely bilingual virtual campus tour, proudly showcasing our beautiful rural ecological features, gardens, and unique open learning spaces.

🎁 Activity 4: Celebration & Gift Exchange

A heartwarming recap of the final milestone exchange where student representatives presented handmade graduation gifts and custom keepsakes to commemorate our partnership milestones.
