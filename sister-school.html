🌍 Sister School Partnership

Our Bilingual Education program proudly connects students globally. Through our official partnership with Señor Tesoro Academy in the Philippines, students at Siang Tian Elementary School practice real-world English communication, share unique cultural traditions, and build lifelong international friendships through engaging interactive video exchanges.

Our Exchange Videos

👋 Student Self-Introductions (Siang Tian)

Our enthusiastic students from Siang Tian Elementary School introduce themselves in English! They share their names, personal interests, and details about their school life here in Changhua County to kick off our global connection.

🇵🇭 Greetings from Señor Tesoro Academy

A wonderful video response from our partner school friends at Señor Tesoro Academy in the Philippines. Their students share greetings, introduce their school culture, and share their excitement for our cross-border partnership.

🌾 Sharing Traditional Taiwanese Snacks

In this video, Siang Tian students introduce delicious, traditional Taiwanese snacks. They explain the unique flavors and stories behind these local treats before packaging them up and sending them to our friends in the Philippines to taste!

🎁 Commemorative Graduation Gifts

Our students explain the special meaning behind the graduation gifts given to our partner school. This visual presentation beautifully captures our mutual gratitude and highlights the deep friendship formed between Siang Tian and Señor Tesoro Academy.
