About the Program

Our Bilingual Initiative proudly extends beyond our physical classroom walls. Through our intentional partnership networks, our elementary students gain rare, real-world opportunities to communicate authentically across borders.

"Connecting our local sparks to make a global flame!"
  • Cross-Cultural Pen Pals: Students write physical letters and digital postcards to practice context-rich reading and writing.
  • Live Video Exchanges: Classrooms connect synchronously to sing cultural songs, play interactive language games, and perform spoken dialogue projects.
  • Shared Collaborative Lessons: Co-teachers cross-plan thematic modules, comparing regional holidays, environments, and school daily routines.
